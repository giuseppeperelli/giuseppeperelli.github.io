About me

NEW! On 1st January 2020 I will join Dipartimento di Ingegneria Informatica, Automatica e Gestionale at Università di Roma, La Sapienza to work with Prof. Giuseppe de Giacomo on his WhiteMech ERC Grant.

I am Postod Researcher at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering of the University of Göteborg, working in the Formal Methods Division with Prof. Nir Piterman.

Prior to this, I was Research Assistant at University of Oxford, working with Prof. Michael Wooldridge. I obtained my PhD at University of Naples "Federico II" working under the supervision of Prof. Aniello Murano. During my PhD, I have been visiting research scholar at Rice University under the supervision of Prof. Moshe Vardi.

Research Interests

My main research interests are in Formal Methods and Artificial Intelligence. My focus is on formal techniques for Strategic Reasoning of Multi-Agent Systems. In particular, I work in the areas of:
  • Automata, Logics and Games for Artificial Intelligence;
  • Verification and Synthesis of Autonomous Agents and Multiagent systems (check out the EVE tool page);
  • Algorithmic Game Theory for Computer Science;
  • Expressiveness and Decidability of Logics for Strategic Reasoning;
  • Knowledge Representation and Reasoning on Games for Artificial Intelligence;
  • Imperfect Information, Epistemic, and Quantitative aspects of Games.
